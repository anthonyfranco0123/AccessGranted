Community

Bus Routes

We created the Bus Routes page to help individuals create a better bus route system in their city. Currently there are too many low-income, working class individuals who often have to rely on public transportation to get to where they need. Often times many individuals end up walking blocks and blocks after their bus ride to get to work or even just home. As a result we have focused a page on fixing this issue by helping communities plan a better bus route system. We currently are focusing on Boise, Idaho but hope to expand to other locations!
